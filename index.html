LiftOff Track and Race Generator

New points are added to the bottom left. Click Close Track to connect start and end point.
Tip: Press STRG and scroll your mouse wheel to make the track editor fit your screen.

Set the space between the markers, the lower the number, the closer together they will be. Press Preview to calculate your settings. After the track has been generated copy the result to a file in a new folder in your track directory. Liftoff reads the tracks on game start, so make sure to do this before starting the game.
